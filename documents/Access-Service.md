**Access Service is an component that enable the LibreSBC communicate to public network (internet) and serve the access functions include:**
* Registrar
* Location Service
* Authentication
* Routing
* Multi-Domain Policy
* Especially, support interconnect to Microsoft Teams Direct Routing
